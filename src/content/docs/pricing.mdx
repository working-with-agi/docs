## What Are AI Credits?

AI credits represent usage of AI features. Each operation consumes credits:

| Operation | Credits |
|---|---|
| AI conversation (1 turn) | ~7 |
| Auto-generated report | ~20 |
| Action item extraction | ~8 |
| Cross-data analysis | ~18 |
| Agenda generation | ~5 |

:::tip[Estimate]
Business plan (10,000 credits/mo) covers ~500 AI conversations + 100 report generations + 200 action extractions per month.
:::

## Credit Top-Up Packs

Need more credits mid-month? Purchase additional packs:
